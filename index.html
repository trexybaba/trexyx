Auxin is a thrilling multiplayer party game that offers intense battles, both locally and online, where treelings compete fiercely for the coveted golden seed. Players will experience the excitement of swinging through beautifully crafted pixel art nature-themed maps using a simple yet challenging liana mechanic. They can shoot, plant, or intercept seeds to outsmart their opponents.

Intense and Skill-Based Battles: Treelings make use of a wide array of moves with their lianas, resulting in dynamic and strategically rich battles filled with breathtaking turnarounds. Every projectile in the game takes the form of seeds that can be strategically planted in the maps for future use.

With over 200 maps across 10 unique themes, each featuring distinctive seeds and hazards, players must tap into their creativity to utilize the environment to their advantage, all in the pursuit of claiming the golden seed and achieving victory.

In Auxin, treelings can face off against each other in Arena mode, engage in thrilling swinging races in Race mode, strategically plant seeds to nurture their sacred trees in Tree Control mode, or compete to score goals in Seedball mode. Additionally, players can team up in Co-op mode to protect nature against destructive forces, with the option to play solo as well.
