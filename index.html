Huedini is a top-down action roguelike game. Play as Huedini, a powerful wizard with the ability to change his colour. By matching your colour, to that of the enemies you can absorb their colour essence. By combining these colour essences into the correct combination, you can cast custom spells.

Switch between Huedini's three colour modes: red, green and blue.

By matching your colour to enemies and their projectiles, you take no damage from them and absorb their colour essence.

By absorbing the correct combinations of colour essences from enemies you can cast powerful spells, that you customise and upgrade as you progress.

All in order to defeat Eldritch Horrors!
